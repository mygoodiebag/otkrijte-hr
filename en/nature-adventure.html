✦ Hiking

Croatian peaks and trails

From Velebit to Biokovo, Croatia offers hundreds of hiking trails for all levels — from easy trekking to demanding alpine climbing.

Velebit
Croatia's largest mountain and a UNESCO biosphere reserve. The Premuzic Trail, Hajducki and Rozanski Kukovi peaks.
📐 1,757 m🛤 Premuzic Trail⭐ UNESCO

Biokovo
A nature park with the famous Skywalk viewpoint at 1,228 m. Spectacular views of the islands and Makarska Riviera.
📐 1,762 m🌉 Skywalk🏖 Makarska
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Ucka
The highest peak in Istria (1,396 m). A nature park with rich flora and views stretching from the Alps to Italy.
📐 1,396 m🌺 Flora🇮🇹 View to Italy
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Dinara
Croatia's highest peak (1,831 m) and its newest nature park. A wild, untouched landscape.
📐 1,831 m🏔 Highest peak HR🌿 Nature park

✦ Cycling

Trails for cyclists

From the former Parenzana railway in Istria to island routes — hundreds of kilometres of well-maintained cycling trails.

Parenzana — Istria
A former narrow-gauge railway converted into a 123-km cycling trail. Tunnels, viaducts and Istrian villages.
🛤 123 km🚇 Tunnels🍷 Wine & olives
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Island of Cres
One of the best islands for cycling. Diverse terrain — from coastal paths to mountain routes with views of Kvarner Bay.
🏝 Island routes🦅 Griffon vulture🌊 Kvarner
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Inland Istria
Rolling hills, vineyards, medieval hilltop towns. Motovun, Groznjan, Oprtalj — cycle through the most beautiful Istrian landscapes.
🏰 Motovun🍇 Vineyards🍄 Truffles
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Island of Hvar
Cycle through lavender fields and olive groves. Trails from Stari Grad to Jelsa with views of the Pakleni Islands.
🌸 Lavender🫓 Olive groves☀ 2,700 h of sun

✦ Rafting & Kayaking

Croatia's wild rivers

Crystal-clear rivers perfect for rafting, kayaking and canyoning. From the adrenaline rapids of Cetina to the calm paddling of Mreznica.

Cetina River
The most popular rafting destination. An 11-km thrilling ride through the canyon — rapids, cliff jumps and crystal-clear sea at the estuary.
🌊 11 km route⚡ Rapids🏊 Cliff jumps
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Zrmanja River
An emerald-green river with cascading waterfalls. Kayaking and rafting through a spectacular canyon.
💚 Emerald🌄 Canyon📸 Photogenic
🏨 Booking 🏠 Airbnb 🌊 Adriatic 📍 Maps
Mreznica River
A calm river ideal for families. Swimming in natural pools, small waterfalls and paddling through green channels.
👪 Families🏊 Swimming🌿 Calm water
