8 National
Parks of Croatia

Croatia's natural heritage — from forested mountains to crystal-clear rivers and magical waterfalls.

Croatia has 8 national parks that together cover more than 1,000 km² of protected nature. Each park is unique — from karst lakes and waterfalls to rocky marine landscapes and wild mountains.

1
Lika
UNESCO Heritage

Plitvice Lakes

16 terraced lakes connected by waterfalls in a surreal primeval forest setting. The most visited park in Croatia and a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1979.

296 km² 16 lakes Waterfalls up to 78 m
