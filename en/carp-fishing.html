Top 10 lakes
for carp fishing

From gravel pits around Zagreb to the reservoir lakes of Slavonia and the coast — these waters attract carp anglers from all over Europe. Trophy specimens up to 40+ kg, catch-and-release policy and excellently organised competitions.

1
⭐ Legend
Slavonia · Dakovo

Lake Borovik

160 ha in the heart of Slavonia — created by damming the River Vuka in 1978. Known as "holy water" among carp anglers, with trophy specimens of 30+ kg. Catch-and-release policy for carp and grass carp.

📏 160 ha
💧 Up to 15 m deep
🔄 Catch & release
🐟 Carp 30+ kg 🎣 Bass fishing ⛺ Camping ⛰ Hiking trails
★★★★★ 4.9 · Borovik Carp Cup

🏙 Near ZG
Zagreb County · Zapresic

Lake Zajarki

A popular gravel pit near Zagreb, formed by gravel extraction. Known for trophy carp of 40+ kg and regular competitions. One of the most attractive waters near the capital.

🐟 Carp 40+ kg 🏆 Competitions 🚗 Near Zagreb
📏 ~30 ha
🏙 20 min from ZG
★★★★★ 4.8 · HCL venue

🏆 League
Zagreb County · Rakitje

Lake Rakitje

A gravel pit known for trophy carp of 30+ kg and regular league competitions. The local fishing club actively participates in the 1st and 2nd Croatian Carp League.

🐟 Carp 30+ kg 🏆 HCL competitions 🐟 Trophy carp 🌿 Natural setting

Primorje · Crikvenica

🏔 Nature park
Dalmatia · Pakostane

Vransko Lake

The largest natural lake in Croatia (~30 km²), a nature park in Dalmatia. Due to its proximity to the sea, it is home to both freshwater and marine species. Year-round fishing.

🏆 Historic achievement

Croatia — World Champions 2024!

At the 24th World Carp and Grass Carp Fishing Championship, held from 4 to 7 September in Nasice, Croatia won the team gold! The competition lasted 72 hours on Lakes Lapovac and Sandor, with 30 national teams from four continents taking part.
